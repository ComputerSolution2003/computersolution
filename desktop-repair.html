Desktop Repair FAQs

Common questions about our desktop repair and custom PC building services in Surat.

How much does a custom gaming PC cost in Surat?

Custom gaming PCs in Surat start from ₹35,000 (entry-level), ₹55,000 (mid-range), and go up to ₹1,50,000+ (high-end RTX builds). The exact cost depends on your CPU, GPU, RAM, SSD, and peripherals choice. We help you choose the best components within your budget.

My desktop is not turning on. Can you fix it?

Yes! "Desktop not turning on" usually means PSU failure, motherboard issue, RAM problem, or loose connections. Our experts diagnose the exact problem for FREE and provide cost-effective solutions. Most cases fixed same day starting ₹500.

Can you upgrade my old desktop PC?

Absolutely! Adding more RAM, switching to SSD, upgrading the GPU, or installing a fresh Windows can make your old PC run like new. We'll inspect your PC for FREE and suggest the best upgrades within your budget.

Do you provide warranty on custom PC builds?

Yes! 1-year warranty on full PC builds and 1-month warranty on individual component replacements. Plus, all individual components come with their original manufacturer warranty (RAM, SSD, GPU, PSU, etc.).

Do you build PCs for businesses and offices?

Yes, we specialize in bulk office PC setups. We provide complete solutions including hardware procurement, Windows + Office licensing, antivirus, networking, and annual maintenance contracts (AMC) for businesses across Surat.

How long does a custom PC build take?

Most custom PC builds are completed within 1-2 days depending on component availability. We test every component thoroughly, install Windows + drivers, and deliver a ready-to-use PC.

What's the difference between HDD and SSD upgrade?

SSD (Solid State Drive) is 5x faster than traditional HDD — boots Windows in 10 seconds vs 60 seconds, opens apps instantly, and improves overall PC performance. We strongly recommend SSD upgrade for any PC older than 3 years.
