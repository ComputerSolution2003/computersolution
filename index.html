Our Story

17 Years of Fixing What Matters

I started Computer Solution back in 2007, from a small shop in Krishna Nagar Society, Adajan. I still remember my cousin bringing me his broken laptop — a Lenovo that wouldn't boot — before I even had a proper shop sign up. We got it running in three hours. That same feeling of handing back a working device to someone who genuinely needed it — that's what still drives me every single day.

Over 17 years, I've repaired laptops for students cramming before exams, recovered data for business owners who thought everything was gone, and built gaming PCs for teenagers in this very neighbourhood. Adajan has grown enormously since 2007, and Computer Solution has grown with it — but the approach hasn't changed: be honest, be fast, and treat every device like it belongs to your own family.

We don't outsource your repair. When you drop your laptop with us, I or my trained team handles it right here in the shop. No middlemen. No delays. No surprises on the bill. That's the promise we've kept for over 4,800 repairs — and counting.
